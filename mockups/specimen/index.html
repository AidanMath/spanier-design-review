Entry No. 06 Process — four steps

How a project becomes a room.

Four steps, in order. Lukas is on the wall on every one of them.

  1. Step 01 — Walkthrough

    A walkthrough, with measurements.

    Lukas walks the property with you, takes photos, and measures with iPad LiDAR for accurate square-footage. No carnival-barker estimates.

  2. Step 02 — Color

    See the color before we touch a wall.

    Sherwin-Williams ColorSnap AR previews on your actual wall, or bring your own swatch. Sample patches encouraged.

  3. Step 03 — Prep + Paint

    Prep, paint, daily clean-up.

    Drop cloths, hardware off, surfaces prepped, two coats unless spec'd. The house is cleaner each evening than we found it.

  4. Step 04 — Walk & Warranty

    A walk-through, in writing.

    Final inspection together. Two-year workmanship warranty — if a coat fails because of how we applied it, we come back and fix it.

Entry No. 10 FAQ — Phase 1 SEO

Things worth asking.

What areas do you serve? +
Central Minnesota — St. Cloud, Sartell, Sauk Rapids, Waite Park, Cold Spring, Avon, Albany, Monticello, Foley, Rice, Becker, Big Lake. Roughly a 60-mile radius from St. Cloud.
How much does a typical project cost? +
Interior repaints start around $2.50–$4.50 per square foot of wall area; full exteriors $4,000–$12,000 depending on size, prep, and condition. We give you a real number after a walkthrough — not a per-hour estimate.
Are you licensed and insured? +
Licensed and insured in Minnesota, plus EPA RRP certified for lead-safe work on pre-1978 homes.
What paint brands do you use? +
Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore are the defaults. We'll work with whatever you specify.
How long does a typical interior repaint take? +
A 1,500 sqft repaint is usually 3–5 working days. Cabinetry runs 5–7 days. Exteriors run 1–2 weeks depending on weather and prep.
Do you do commercial work? +
Yes — offices, dental and medical, restaurants, retail. Most commercial work is scheduled after-hours or weekends so you don't lose business days.
Is there a warranty? +
Two-year workmanship warranty in writing. If a coat fails because of how we applied it, we come back and fix it.
