About / Origin

A four-painter studio out of St. Cloud.

Spanier Painting has been the painter Central Minnesota families recommend by name since 2014. Lukas Spanier started the company at 19 — a one-man crew, one ladder, a willingness to do the prep no one else wanted to. Twelve years later, Spanier Painting is a four-painter studio working across the St. Cloud region on everything from heritage Queen Annes to two-day commercial turnarounds. Painted by hand. Built to last.

Capabilities / Scope of Work

Services

01 / 06

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, trim, doors. Two-coat over a primed surface, eggshell on walls and semi-gloss on trim by default; flat ceilings unless specified. Drop cloths and protected hardware standard.

Most Requested
02 / 06

Exterior Painting

Siding, soffits, fascia, trim, garages. Power-washed, scraped, primed and back-rolled; alkyd-hybrid on woodwork, elastomeric where bridging is required.

Full-season
03 / 06

Cabinet Refinishing

Kitchens, vanities, built-ins. Doors and drawer fronts removed and finished off-site for an even, dust-free coat; conversion varnish or pigmented lacquer depending on the cabinetry.

High-demand
04 / 06

Heritage / Historic Homes

Older homes, lead-safe practice, color-matching original paint, plaster repair. EPA RRP certified. Patient detail work on the houses that earned the detail.

EPA RRP

Method / Four Clauses

How We Work

01

Walkthrough Day 1 · on-site

Lukas walks the property with you, takes photos, and measures with iPad LiDAR for accurate square-footage. No per-hour guessing — every quote is built off real measurements and real condition.

02

Color Pre-start · AR-assisted

Sherwin-Williams ColorSnap AR previews on your wall, or bring your own swatch. Sample patches optional. See your color before we touch a wall — we'll talk you out of one we know you'd regret.

03

Prep + paint Job days · on-site

Drop cloths down, hardware off, surfaces prepped, two coats unless spec'd otherwise. Daily clean-up. Furniture returned each night. No paint debris in carpet or on hardware.

04

Walk-through + warranty Final day · together

Final inspection together. Anything flagged at walk-through gets fixed before we mark complete. 2-year workmanship warranty in writing — if a coat fails because of how we applied it, we come back.

Reference / Questions On File

Frequently Asked.

SPANIER PAINTING — REFERENCE QUESTIONS — REV. 2026.01 — ST. CLOUD, MN

Q.01

What areas do you serve?

Central Minnesota — St. Cloud, Sartell, Sauk Rapids, Waite Park, Cold Spring, Avon, Albany, Monticello, Foley, Rice, Becker, Big Lake. Roughly a 60-mile radius from St. Cloud.

Q.02

How much does a typical project cost?

Interior repaints start around $2.50–$4.50/sqft of wall area; full exteriors $4,000–$12,000 depending on size, prep, and condition. We give you a real number after a walkthrough — not a per-hour estimate.

Q.03

Do you give free estimates?

Yes. A walkthrough, photos, measurements, and a written quote — at no cost.

Q.04

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — licensed and insured in Minnesota, plus EPA RRP certified for lead-safe work on pre-1978 homes.

Q.05

What paint brands do you use?

Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore are the defaults. We'll work with whatever you specify.

Q.06

How long does a typical interior repaint take?

A 1,500 sqft repaint is usually 3–5 working days. Cabinetry runs 5–7 days. Exteriors run 1–2 weeks depending on weather and prep.

Q.07

Do you do commercial work?

Yes — offices, dental and medical, restaurants, retail. Most commercial work is scheduled after-hours or weekends so you don't lose business days.

Q.08

Is there a warranty?

Two-year workmanship warranty in writing. If a coat fails because of how we applied it, we come back and fix it.

SPANIER PAINTING — WRITTEN PROMISES — REV. 2026.01 — ST. CLOUD, MN 56301

01

We will show up on time and communicate before, during, and after the project. If a day is delayed due to weather or material delivery, you will know before 7 AM.

02

We will prepare every surface to the standard required by the product manufacturer and the end-use environment — not to the minimum that looks passable at quote time.

03

We will use only the products specified in the quote — no substitutions without your written approval. Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore are the defaults.

04

We will leave the site clean — drop cloths removed, floors swept, fixtures rehung, and furniture returned each day. No paint debris in carpet or on hardware.

05

We will walk the finished work with you before marking the project complete and address any items identified at walk-through at no additional charge.

06

We will stand behind the work with a two-year workmanship warranty in writing. If a coat fails because of how we applied it, we come back and fix it — no charge, no debate.
