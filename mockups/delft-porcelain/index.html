IV Services
Services

What we do.

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, trim, doors. Two coats over a primed surface. Eggshell on walls, semi-gloss on trim, flat ceilings unless specified. Drop cloths and protected hardware standard.

Eggshell · Semi-gloss · Flat · 2-coat

Exterior Painting

Siding, soffits, fascia, trim, garages. Power-washed, scraped, primed and back-rolled. Alkyd-hybrid on woodwork, elastomeric where bridging is required.

Alkyd-hybrid · Elastomeric · Back-rolled

Cabinet Refinishing

Kitchens, vanities, built-ins. Doors and drawer fronts removed and finished off-site for an even, dust-free coat. Conversion varnish or pigmented lacquer depending on the cabinetry.

Conversion varnish · Pigmented lacquer

Heritage / Historic Homes

Older homes, lead-safe practice, color-matching original paint, plaster repair. EPA RRP certified. Patient detail work on profiles no catalogue reproduces.

EPA RRP Certified · Lead-safe · Plaster repair

Decks + Fences

Stain, seal, refresh. Penetrating oils, semi-transparent stains, or solid-body coatings depending on age and exposure. Built for the Minnesota freeze-thaw cycle.

Penetrating oil · Semi-transparent · Solid body

Specialty Coatings

Garage floors (epoxy), commercial walls, fire-rated coatings, anti-graffiti finishes, kitchen-safe cabinetry refresh.

Epoxy · Fire-rated · Anti-graffiti

V Process
How We Work

The Process,
in Four Movements

I

Walkthrough

Lukas walks the property with you, takes photos, and measures with iPad LiDAR for accurate square-footage. No per-hour guesswork — a real number after a real walk-through.

II

Color

Sherwin-Williams ColorSnap AR previews on your wall, or bring your own swatch. Sample patches optional. See the colour before we touch a wall.

III

Prep + Paint

Drop cloths, hardware off, surfaces prepped, two coats unless spec'd otherwise. Daily clean-up. One room at a time, completely, never reversed.

IV

Walk-through + Warranty

Final inspection together. Two-year workmanship warranty in writing — if a coat fails because of how we applied it, we come back and fix it.

Questions Often Asked

A Pamphlet of
Plain Answers

I. What areas do you serve?
Central Minnesota — St. Cloud, Sartell, Sauk Rapids, Waite Park, Cold Spring, Avon, Albany, Monticello, Foley, Rice, Becker, Big Lake. Roughly a 60-mile radius from St. Cloud.
II. How much does a typical project cost?
Interior repaints start around $2.50–$4.50/sqft of wall area; full exteriors $4,000–$12,000 depending on size, prep, and condition. We give you a real number after a walkthrough — not a per-hour estimate.
III. Do you give free estimates?
Yes. A walkthrough, photos, measurements, and a written quote — at no cost.
IV. Are you licensed and insured?
Yes — licensed and insured in Minnesota, plus EPA RRP certified for lead-safe work on pre-1978 homes.
V. What paint brands do you use?
Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore are the defaults. We'll work with whatever you specify.
VI. How long does a typical interior repaint take?
A 1,500 sqft repaint is usually 3–5 working days. Cabinetry runs 5–7 days. Exteriors run 1–2 weeks depending on weather and prep.
VII. Do you do commercial work?
Yes — offices, dental and medical, restaurants, retail. Most commercial work is scheduled after-hours or weekends so you don't lose business days.
VIII. Is there a warranty?
Two-year workmanship warranty in writing. If a coat fails because of how we applied it, we come back and fix it.
