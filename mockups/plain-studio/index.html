About

Spanier Painting has been the painter Central Minnesota families recommend by name since 2014. Lukas Spanier started the company at 19 — a one-man crew, one ladder, a willingness to do the prep no one else wanted to. Twelve years later, Spanier Painting is a four-painter studio working across the St. Cloud region on everything from heritage Queen Annes to two-day commercial turnarounds.

FAQ

  • What areas do you serve?

    Central Minnesota — St. Cloud, Sartell, Sauk Rapids, Waite Park, Cold Spring, Avon, Albany, Monticello. Roughly a 60-mile radius from St. Cloud.

  • How much does a typical project cost?

    Interior repaints start around $2.50–$4.50/sqft of wall area; full exteriors $4,000–$12,000 depending on size, prep, and condition. Real number after a walkthrough — not a per-hour estimate.

  • Are you licensed and insured?

    Yes — licensed and insured in Minnesota, plus EPA RRP certified for lead-safe work on pre-1978 homes.

  • Is there a warranty?

    Two-year workmanship warranty in writing. If a coat fails because of how we applied it, we come back and fix it.
