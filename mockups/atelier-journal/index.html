01 / The work

The painter Central
Minnesota families
recommend by name.

Spanier Painting started in 2014 — one painter, one ladder, and the willingness to do the prep nobody else wanted to. Twelve years later it's a four-painter studio working across St. Cloud, Sartell, Sauk Rapids, and the surrounding region.

Interior repaints, full exteriors, cabinet refinishing, heritage homes, decks, and commercial buildouts. Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore as defaults. Two coats over a primed surface as standard. Drop cloths down, hardware off, daily clean-up — every job, no exceptions.

Licensed and insured in Minnesota. EPA RRP certified for lead-safe work on pre-1978 homes. Two-year workmanship warranty in writing. Lukas is on every job — walking the property with you on day one and back at the end to inspect the last cut line.

02 / Approach

How a Spanier job goes.

Prep + two coats over a primed surface

Standard interior build

Spackle, sand, prime, two finish coats. Eggshell on walls, semi-gloss on trim, flat on ceilings unless you specify otherwise. Drop cloths down, hardware off, every job — no exceptions.

Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore as defaults. We'll work with whatever you specify.

What comes standard on every job

  • Walls spackled, sanded, and primed before any finish coat goes on
  • Two coats — eggshell on walls, semi-gloss on trim, flat on ceilings
  • Drop cloths down, hardware off, switch plates removed and labeled
  • ColorSnap AR preview or a real sample patch before you commit
  • iPad LiDAR measurements — accurate square footage, written quote
  • Daily clean-up — your house stays your house each evening
  • Lukas on every job — owner walks the property on day one and day done
  • Two-year workmanship warranty — in writing, no fine print

05 / Process

From walkthrough to written warranty.

  1. Walkthrough

    Lukas walks the property with you, takes photographs of every surface, and measures with iPad LiDAR for accurate square-footage. No estimating tape, no guessing. You receive a written quote, not a per-hour figure.

    iPad LiDAR · room scan

  2. Color

    Sherwin-Williams ColorSnap AR previews on your wall before the first brushstroke, or bring your own swatch. Sample patches optional and encouraged — colors read differently under Minnesota winter light than under summer sun.

    SW ColorSnap AR · in-room preview

  3. Prep + paint

    Drop cloths down, hardware off, surfaces prepped to spec, two coats applied unless the job calls for more. Daily clean-up — your house stays your house after we leave each day.

    2-coat standard · daily clean-up

  4. Walkthrough + warranty

    Final walkthrough together. We mark anything you want re-touched and fix it before the truck leaves. A two-year workmanship warranty in writing — if a coat fails because of how we applied it, we come back.

    2-yr warranty · in writing

08 / FAQ

Common questions, plainly answered.

01

What areas do you serve?

Central Minnesota — St. Cloud, Sartell, Sauk Rapids, Waite Park, Cold Spring, Avon, Albany, Monticello, Foley, Rice, Becker, Big Lake. Roughly a 60-mile radius from St. Cloud.

02

How much does a typical project cost?

Interior repaints start around $2.50–$4.50 per square foot of wall area. Full exteriors run $4,000–$12,000 depending on size, prep, and condition. We give you a real number after a walkthrough — not a per-hour estimate.

03

Do you give estimates at no cost?

Yes. A walkthrough, photographs, LiDAR measurements, and a written quote — at no cost to you.

04

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — licensed and insured in Minnesota, plus EPA RRP certified for lead-safe work on pre-1978 homes. Certificates supplied on request.

05

What paint brands do you use?

Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore as defaults. We'll work with whatever you specify — Farrow & Ball, Cabot, conversion varnish from any reputable supplier.

06

How long does a typical interior repaint take?

A 1,500 sqft repaint is usually 3–5 working days. Cabinetry runs 5–7 days. Exteriors run 1–2 weeks depending on weather and prep condition.

07

Do you do commercial work?

Yes — offices, dental and medical, restaurants, retail. Most commercial work is scheduled after-hours or weekends so you don't lose business days.

08

Is there a warranty?

Two-year workmanship warranty in writing. If a coat fails because of how we applied it, we come back and fix it — no fine print.
